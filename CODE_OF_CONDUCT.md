# Code of Conduct

This repository expects direct, useful, and respectful technical behavior.

## Expected Behavior

- stay on topic
- be specific about bugs, behavior, or documentation gaps
- write in a way that helps maintainers and other readers act on the feedback
- disagree with the content, not with people

## Unacceptable Behavior

- insults, mockery, humiliation, harassment, or personal attacks
- hostile, sarcastic, or baiting comments
- spam, noise, or low-effort criticism with no useful direction
- culture-war, political, or unrelated arguments
- demands for unpaid support or entitled behavior

## Enforcement

The repository owner may edit, hide, delete, lock, or ignore comments, issues, and pull requests that do not help improve the repository. Repeated poor behavior may lead to blocks or reporting through the hosting platform.
